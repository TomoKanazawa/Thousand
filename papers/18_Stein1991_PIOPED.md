## Abstract
Abstract unavailable verbatim without paywall access for the 1991 paper. The 1991 PIOPED publication characterized presenting clinical features (dyspnea, pleuritic chest pain, tachypnea) of patients with angiographically confirmed PE who had no prior cardiopulmonary disease, establishing the symptom prevalence base rates still cited today.

## Key numbers (1995 paper)
- 1.0% prevalence of acute PE among hospitalized patients (526/51,645)

## Why this matters for Andy
PE is the canonical "leading missed dx" in inpatient malpractice and meta-analytic data (see #04). PIOPED provides the clinical base rates for any PE-detection module.
