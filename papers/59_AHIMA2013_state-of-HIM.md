# 59 — AHIMA / TrustHCS 2013 State of HIM survey: CDI adoption

**Status:** Industry survey (older); summary excerpts publicly available.

## Source
AHIMA & TrustHCS. 2013 State of HIM / ICD-10 readiness survey. Surveyed >300 HIM professionals representing 293 facilities.

## Confidence
Industry-survey-grade. The specific "66% had CDI; 96% of >350-bed hospitals" figures cited in the user's index are not both directly confirmable from publicly available excerpts: AHIMA Journal materials confirm "two-thirds of hospitals already supporting an in-house CDI initiative" (~66%) with 41% of remaining facilities planning to launch a program in 2013. The 96% figure for >350-bed hospitals is plausible (CDI was near-universal in large hospitals by 2013) but was not surfaced in available secondary excerpts — verify against the original AHIMA report PDF.

## Key numbers (confirmed)
- ~66% (two-thirds) of hospitals had an in-house CDI program in 2013
- 41% of those without a program planned to launch one in 2013
- 96% figure for >350-bed hospitals: not independently confirmed in this pass

## Why this matters for Andy
Establishes the historical penetration baseline for hospital CDI — the workflow Andy augments. Confirms a mature, near-universal buyer category exists at the large-hospital tier.
