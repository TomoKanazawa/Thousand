# 55 — ACDIS 2024 CDI Week Industry Overview Survey

**Status:** Industry survey; report freely available from ACDIS.

## Source
ACDIS (Association of Clinical Documentation Integrity Specialists). 2024 CDI Week Industry Overview Survey. 14th annual survey; >800 respondents.

## Confidence
Industry-association survey; self-reported by CDI practitioners. Useful for directional benchmarks, not peer-reviewed.

## URL
- https://acdis.org/cdi-week/2024-cdi-week-industry-overview-survey
- https://acdis.org/articles/2024-cdi-week-industry-overview-survey-report-available-now

## Key numbers (from ACDIS published summary)
- >800 respondents; 14th annual
- 36.13% acute-care hospital (vs 40.06% in 2023) — CDI roles diversifying beyond inpatient
- 70.45% hold RN credential; 70.32% hold CCDS
- 73.44% report query response rate in the 91–100% range
- 29.69% query 31–40% of cases reviewed
- ~64% of CDI programs involved in denials management
- 47.16% review pediatric cases (inpatient or outpatient)

## Why this matters for Andy
Defines who the CDI buyer is, what their query workflow looks like, and where their workload is shifting (outpatient, denials, risk adjustment) — all entry points Andy can plug into.
